Titans of the Cosmos

So some interesting facts about space, the Andromeda Galaxy will eventually collide with our galaxy, the Milky Way, in around 4.5 billion years! While people might think this would be a chaotic event throwing stars, colliding planets, etc... its actually predicted that the stars in each respective galaxy are so far apart from eachother, there will be little to no impact on the positioning of them once the merger starts. The biggest impacts will be around the core, near the supermassive black hole Saggitarius A*, since the stars are much closer together and both supermassive black holes will merge into one.
